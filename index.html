THE ARTIST AS ARCHAEOLOGIST AND WHY THATS A BAD IDEA

Looking at work by Saskia Van Imhoff and Alicja Kwade at De Appel arts centre, Amsterdam, March 2016

A lot of artist these days present themselves as a sort of 'discoverers' of contemporary culture. They present there findings as precious artifacts, powerful and contemplative in their meaningless ambiguity. A pseudo scientific objectivity gives these wornout acts of appropriation a sort of genteel legitimate aura. In this video we look at the work of 2 of these  archeo-artists: we strongly disaprove of the work -even within the genre of archaeo-art it seems very weak- and we also explain why a government funded institute with its army of curators pays attention to this genre. Archaeo art is silly, but it is part of our world and we keep running in to archaeo-art when we walk into museums and project spaces. This videoclip is the result of taking a videocamera during one of these visits.
