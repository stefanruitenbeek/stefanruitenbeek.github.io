Berlin Studio Portraits

Sandra.
Sandra was one of the best people I photographed. Attitude slightly flegmatic, a sort of unstirred, impertubable presence, but also enthousiastic and exhuberent in a peculiar way, almost like a child, or a duckling. She somehow made my concept, or my 'method' work. Like Lucien Freuds approach to painting also becomes stronger when he portraits a certain kind of person.
